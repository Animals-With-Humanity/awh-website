Our Wings

We approach welfare holistically through these pillars.

On-Field Unit

Core Welfare

The backbone of AWH’s on-ground impact. Handles daily rescue calls, road-accident emergencies, medical assistance, sterilisation and vaccination support, foster coordination, rehabilitation, and legal intervention in cruelty cases, including coordination with authorities and case follow-ups.

Events, Outreach & Campaigns

Kaaya designs and executes large-scale events, awareness campaigns, humane education projects, and public engagements. It brings citizens together to engage, learn, participate in animal–human bonding, and actively contribute toward building a compassionate and conscious city.

Shop Sustainable

Vastu promotes eco-conscious living through sustainable merchandise and upcycling initiatives. The wing actively creates livelihood opportunities for marginalised women artisan, while supporting animal welfare through it's proceeds.

Animal Adoption & Caretaking

Paltu promotes the adoption of Indie (Desi) and rescued animals while supporting caretaking of community animals. The wing advocates responsible animal caretaking and works closely with adopters and caregivers to build a strong, ethical, inclusive & symbiotic animal-human ecosystem.

What we do?

Day in and day out, this is how we serve the city.

Animal Rescue

We intervene to rescue animals in distress that are living on the streets, providing immediate first aid.

Awareness

We conduct sensitization programs in schools, colleges, residential societies, and with government authorities to promote coexistence.

Fostering & Adoption

We offer temporary shelter for distressed animals during their recovery period and work towards securing permanent homes for them.

Sterilisation

We collaborate with local administrative bodies to facilitate the sterilization of community dogs and cats.

Vaccination

We provide free rabies vaccination and vaccinations for other diseases at affordable cost in companion animals.

Legal Intervention

We address and help in cases of animal cruelty through legal means.

Awards & Recognition

Honored for our commitment to welfare.

Madhya Pradesh Excellence Award (2025)

Conferred at Minto Hall, Bhopal, by the Hon’ble Chief Minister of Madhya Pradesh, Dr. Mohan Yadav, for sustained and impactful work in the animal welfare sector, including rescue, rehabilitation, adoption advocacy, community animal care, and ethical awareness initiatives across Madhya Pradesh.

Devi Ahilyabai Award (2024)

Felicitated by Barkatullah University for notable achievements in animal welfare, social service, and community-led impact, acknowledging long-term contribution to ethical practices, awareness-building, and animal protection efforts.

Yoddha Awards (2023)

Presented at Minto Hall by Shri Vishwas Sarang, recognising significant social impact through animal welfare initiatives, grassroots action, public engagement, and consistent efforts toward protecting animals and promoting compassion-driven community change.
